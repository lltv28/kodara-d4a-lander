Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from ChatGPT or a regular chatbot?

Every chatbot, including ChatGPT, has the same flaw: they wait. Your client has to know the right question to ask, and when they ask, they get generic advice that sounds like a robot wrote it. Your AI works the other way. We train it on your content, your voice, and the way you think, and it goes first. It asks your leads the right questions, the same ones you'd ask a new client. It's like having your brain in a box your clients keep on their desk.

Won't a cheap first offer hurt my main, high-priced offer?

It feeds it. The small first offer is how a stranger first gets their foot in the door and tries your work. The people who buy at $20 are the ones who trust you enough to buy at $20,000 later. In their mind it's just an upgrade, and upgrades are an easy yes.

What if I don't have enough people coming in?

That's Step 3: we bring you the people. We start by waking up the old contacts already in your email list, which is usually the first money you see. Then we bring in new people with sales pages, social posts, and paid ads.

How do you get me clients?

We build the whole marketing system around your AI: the sales page, the posts from your avatar, the wake-up emails for your old contacts, and paid ads once the rest is working. We fix the gaps in what you're already doing, then bring in new people on top, using the same systems we've used to make over $100 million for our clients.

How much of my time does this take?

Very little, because we do it for you. All we ask is about one hour a week: you give our team feedback and share anything new that keeps your AI up to date. The whole point is a business that can run without you.

What if I'm not technical?

You don't need to be. You don't touch funnels, automations, ad tools, or AI tools. Our team handles all of it. Your job is to be the expert. Our job is to grow you.

Who owns the AI, and what happens to my data?

You own the AI version of you that we build. It's trained on your work alone. Your content and your clients' data stay yours.

What kinds of businesses does this work for?

Any expert whose personal time is the bottleneck: coaches, consultants, doctors, financial advisors, mortgage brokers, retirement planners, healthcare practitioners. If your business depends on you being personally available to make money, it's built for you.
