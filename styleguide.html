Kodara design system

Tokens live in tokens.css and are inlined into index.html and front-door-app-embed.html by node build-cf.mjs. Edit the tokens file, never the inlined copies. The in-app demo UI additionally keeps its own cool-gray product neutrals (--a900…--a100, local to the embed).

Color

Neutrals · warm ink
--inktext
--ink-2body copy
--ink-3muted
--surfacetint sections
--line-2borders
Accent
--accentbrand green
--accent-inktext on light
--accent-mintaccents on dark
--accent-softfills
--darkdark sections

In the embed, the whole accent family is derived from --accent via color-mix() / relative color, so industry themes are one color + one orb image.

Type

--fs-displayDisplay
--fs-h2Heading two
--fs-h3Heading three
--fs-titleCard title
--fs-leadLead paragraph for section subheads.
--fs-bodyBody copy — 22px at default root.
--fs-smSupporting copy and list items.
--fs-labelMicrolabel · mono uppercase

Display = Instrument Serif. Body = Instrument Sans. Mono = IBM Plex Mono, reserved for the microlabel recipe (eyebrows, chips, player tags) — the strongest brand signature, use sparingly.

Section rhythm uses the fluid semantic tokens in index.html (--space-section, --space-block, …) built on this scale. Breakpoints (not tokenizable): 600 / 760 / 860 px max-width.

Radius

--r-sm10 · icons, inputs
--r-md12 · cards, buttons
--r-lg16 · media, glass
--r-xl24 · notifications
--r-pillpills, CTAs

Device bezels (48/54px) stay component-local by design.

Buttons

Primary = --grad-btn + --shadow-accent (hover: --shadow-accent-lift). The embed's in-app CTAs share the same gradient and --shadow-accent-sm at --r-md. One primary per viewport; use ghost/text links for section closers.

Cards

Flat surface

White, --line-2 border, --r-md. Timeline + case-study cards.

Glass surface

Translucent + blur, --r-lg. In-app demo cards and iOS nudges only.

Dark section

Gradient --dark → --dark-2 via .section.dark; accents switch to --accent-mint.

Motion

--ease-out for reveals and most transitions, --ease-spring for the notification stack, --ease-move for the demo's cursor travel. Durations: --dur-fast (hover) / --dur-base (state) / --dur-slow (reveals). Both pages honor prefers-reduced-motion: reveals render visible, the nudge carousel doesn't auto-cycle, and the embed collapses its eased scrolling.
